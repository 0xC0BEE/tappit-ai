# Tappit AI: Future Roadmap

This document outlines the strategic roadmap for Tappit AI, building upon the successful beta launch. Our vision is to evolve from a digital business card into an indispensable, AI-powered relationship management platform for professionals and teams.

---

### Immediate Next Steps (Post-Beta)

*   **Objective:** Stabilize, Listen, and Iterate.
*   **Key Initiatives:**
    *   **[Product] Build Full Intro Site:** Execute on the `intro_site_plan.md` to create a high-converting marketing presence.
    *   **[Product] Robust Feedback System:** Implement an in-app feedback mechanism beyond the current modal to systematically collect and analyze user suggestions and bug reports from beta testers.
    *   **[Engineering] Production Backend:** Replace the mock Supabase client with a full production-ready backend, including secure authentication, a scalable database, and optimized edge functions.
    *   **[Growth] Analyze Beta Metrics:** Use PostHog data to understand the user journey, identify drop-off points, and measure feature engagement.

---

### Q1 2026: Foundation & Growth

*   **Objective:** Reach feature parity with competitors on core platform offerings and begin monetizing our unique strengths.
*   **Key Initiatives:**
    *   **[Product] Physical NFC Cards:**
        *   Establish a partnership with a sustainable manufacturer for bamboo NFC cards.
        *   Build an e-commerce flow within the app for users to design and order their physical cards.
    *   **[Engineering] Native Mobile Apps (iOS & Android):**
        *   Begin development using Expo to wrap our existing web codebase.
        *   Implement native-only features: background NFC reading, native share extensions, and push notifications.
    *   **[Product] CRM Integrations:**
        *   Develop integrations to sync contacts and interactions with major CRMs (HubSpot, Salesforce). This is critical for enterprise adoption.

---

### Q2 2026: Scaling AI & Teams

*   **Objective:** Double down on our AI moat and expand our enterprise offerings.
*   **Key Initiatives:**
    *   **[Product] Advanced AI Gems:**
        *   **"Meeting Prep" Gem:** An AI agent that pulls a contact's latest LinkedIn posts, news articles, and company updates to provide talking points before a meeting.
        *   **"Relationship Score" Gem:** An evolving score that analyzes the frequency and quality of interactions to suggest when it's time to reconnect.
    *   **[Product] Proactive AI Nudges:**
        *   Implement the "AI Nudge" feature fully with push notifications.
        *   e.g., "You haven't spoken to Jane Doe in 3 months. Here's an article their company just published you could mention."
    *   **[Engineering] V2 Team Dashboard:**
        *   Build out more advanced team analytics, including leaderboards, connection mapping, and ROI tracking on networking efforts.

---

### Long-Term Vision (H2 2026 & Beyond)

*   **Objective:** Become the category leader in intelligent relationship management.
*   **Key Initiatives:**
    *   **Conversational AI:** Develop a chat-based interface where users can manage their network by talking to the Tappit AI. ("Show me all my contacts in San Francisco," "Draft a follow-up to my meeting with John Smith.")
    *   **Enterprise-Grade Security:** Achieve SOC 2 compliance to further penetrate the enterprise market.
    *   **Wearable Integration:** Build an Apple Watch and Wear OS app for even faster, more seamless card sharing.
    *   **Ecosystem Expansion:** Develop a public API to allow third-party developers to build their own "Gems" for the AI Studio.
