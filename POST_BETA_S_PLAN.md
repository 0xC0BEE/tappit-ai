# Tappit AI: Post-Beta Sprint Plan

This document outlines the strategic sprints to transition Tappit AI from a successful beta into a market-leading, production-ready application. It is the synthesis of our competitor analysis and future roadmap.

**Goal:** Establish a public presence to convert beta buzz into new users and replace mock systems with production infrastructure.
